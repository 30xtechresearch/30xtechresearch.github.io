MTF + Macro Gate

Every T1–T3 entry must pass the Multi-Timeframe Confluence Gate — 4+ votes across 15m/1h/4h/1d — plus 8 macro indexes (SPX, DXY, CMC20). Weak setups are auto-blocked.

03
AI Confirmation

T1 and T8 signals go through Claude AI review. SL/TP are automatically adjusted using live liquidation cluster data. Walk-Forward Optimiser tunes params every 7 days.

04
Bot Alert

Approved signal delivered instantly to your private Telegram bot with entry, liq-adjusted SL/TP, RR ratio, MTF vote score, Ichimoku bias, VWAP level, and full index confluence.

Create Your Bot

3
Send Bot Token to Admin

Then: /baas chat <your_uid> <chat_id>
4
Go Live
Signals will be delivered directly to your bot's chat as they fire. No further setup needed.
Type 2 — WL + CopyTrade + Resell
How it works: Your own bot receives signals AND mirrors trades directly to your exchange account (CopyTrade). You can also resell signal access from your bot to your own subscribers.
1
Subscribe to WL + CopyTrade Plan
Choose WL + CopyTrade — Full or CopyTrade — Signals Mirror from the Plans tab. Paystack activates instantly.
2
Create Your Bot via @BotFather
Get your bot token → send to admin via: /baas token <uid> <token>
Then set your chat: /baas chat <uid> <chat_id>
3
Connect Exchange for CopyTrade
Admin runs: /baas ctkeys <uid> <exchange> <api_key> <api_secret>
Supported: Bybit, Bitget, Binance, OKX, Kraken, MEXC
4
Configure & Consent
Admin sets market type: /baas ctconfig <uid> market_type spot
Then confirms consent: /baas ctconsent <uid>
5
Resell to Your Subscribers
Your bot can resell signal subscriptions to your own users using the built-in WL user management system. Admin sets up: /baas synccommands on your bot.
